## About SkillSpotter

SkillSpotter is a UK-based two-sided marketplace that connects customers with verified local service providers for on-demand and scheduled services.

The platform helps customers discover, compare, and book skilled professionals within their local area while providing service providers with the tools needed to establish their presence, attract customers, and grow their business.

The initial focus is on barbering and grooming services, with opportunities to expand into additional local service categories.

## Problem Statement

Finding reliable local service providers remains a challenge for many consumers.

Customers often struggle with:

* Identifying trusted professionals nearby
* Verifying provider credibility
* Comparing available service options
* Managing bookings efficiently

Service providers face challenges such as:

* Limited visibility to potential customers
* Difficulty acquiring new clients consistently
* Dependence on referrals and social media
* Lack of accessible digital business tools

## Solution

SkillSpotter bridges the gap between local demand and skilled professionals through a location-based marketplace.

### For Customers

* Discover providers within their postcode radius
* Browse available services
* Compare local professionals
* Book appointments seamlessly

### For Service Providers

* Create and manage professional profiles
* List available services
* Increase local visibility
* Receive and manage bookings
* Grow their customer base

## Marketplace Model

SkillSpotter operates as a commission-based marketplace.

Rather than relying on recurring subscription fees, the platform generates value by facilitating successful transactions between customers and service providers.
